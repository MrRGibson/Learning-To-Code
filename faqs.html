Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs):

Why do activities/events need to be reviewed by an administrator?

Activities and events that are submitted using the form are reviewed by administrators to make sure the activities/events are legitimate.

How long does it take an administrator to review submissions?

The administrators seek to review all events submitted within 2 working days, however, there may be times where this may take long so please be patient. If you are worried that your event was not successfully submitted then feel free to contact us using our contact form
