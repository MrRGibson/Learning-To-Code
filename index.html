Extracurricular Activities

Whilst it is important to be focused at work, it is also important to have a good work-life balance. This includes having interests and hobbies out-side of work.

The purpose of this collection of web pages is to provide colleagues with the opportunity to advertise extracurricular activities that they are taking part in and to enable colleagues to find new activities that may interest them.
